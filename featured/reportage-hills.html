R. Hills - Project Fact Sheet

Project Summary

R.Hills is a community capturing a refined blend of elegance and functionality, tailored to embody the contemporary lifestyle whilst respecting timeless design principles.

Its logo and modern unique facade design reflect the perfect marriage of boldness, sophistication and beauty of an unmistakable hallmark of upscale signature living.

Rooted in sustainable choices and designed for contemporary living, R. Hills stands as a modern statement of style, perfectly attuned to the values and aesthetics of this era.
