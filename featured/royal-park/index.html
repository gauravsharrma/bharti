Royal Park – Fact Sheet

Project Overview

Royal Park is in MASDAR City, Abu Dhabi, offering high-end residential units such as Townhouses, Penthouses, and Apartments. It’s a modern, sustainable community built to the highest standards, surrounded by parks, schools, mosques, and community retails.

Project Highlights

  • Total Units: 844
  • 45 Four-Bedroom Townhouses (G+2)
  • 42 Four-Bedroom Penthouses
  • 522 One-Bedroom Apartments
  • 170 Two-Bedroom Apartments
  • 65 Four-Bedroom Apartments
  • Plot Area: 27,047.38 sq.m.
  • Total Height: 33 m
  • Levels: 2 Basement + Ground + 8 Floors
  • Type: Residential Community (6 clusters)

Amenities & Services

  • Private Parking for Each Unit
  • Swimming Pools for Adults & Kids
  • Gymnasium
  • Kids Play Areas
  • Shaded Seating Areas, Giant Chess
  • Landscaped & BBQ Areas

Unit Features

  • Private Balconies & Terraces
  • Built-in Wardrobes
  • Double-Glazed Windows
  • Fully Tiled Bathrooms with Vanity Units
  • Satellite Antenna & Fiber Optic Internet
  • Central A/C
  • Optional Internal Elevator in Townhouses

Location Advantages

  • 3 mins – Zayed University AI
  • 5 mins – Masdar Solar Farm
  • 10 mins – Masdar Park & Abu Dhabi Int. Airport
