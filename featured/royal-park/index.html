Royal Park – Masdar City, Abu Dhabi

Overview

Royal Park is a prestigious residential project located in Masdar City, Abu Dhabi, offering high-end residential units designed for luxury living. The development features 6 clusters comprising a total of 844 units, including townhouses, penthouses, and apartments — all built to international standards. The community is close to major Abu Dhabi attractions and surrounded by essential facilities like schools, mosques, parks, and retail.
