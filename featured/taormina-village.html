Taormina Village

Project Details

Taormina Village is in Dubailand, offering high-end residential townhouses with luxury lifestyle benefits, close proximity to attractions, and community services.

It emphasizes sustainability and green development with extensive landscaping and eco-friendly policies.

Taormina includes 838 townhouses and villas built to international standards of quality and design.

  • Type: Residential Townhouses
  • Plot Area: 148,638.02 sq.m
  • Total Units: 838 villas
