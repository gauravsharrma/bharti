Marlin 2 – Fact Sheet

🏢 Project Overview

Designed by the Ocean — Inspired by the rhythm of the waves, Marlin 2 is a sculptural architectural masterpiece. Flowing with elegance and light, it merges nature and innovation. It is more than a building — it is an experience.

Located in Al Reem Island, Abu Dhabi, this tower offers breathtaking waterfront views, sophisticated interiors, and a lifestyle rooted in tranquility and elegance.

📐 Project Details

  • Type: Residential
  • Plot Area: 4,067 sq.m
  • Total Height: 78.40 m
  • Total Units: 164
  • Levels: Ground + 3 Podiums + 16 Floors
  • Private Parkings: 276
