Verdana – Fact Sheet

Project Overview

Verdana is located in Dubai Investments Park (DIP), offering high-end residential townhouses surrounded by parks, schools, mosques, and retail. Designed with eco-friendliness in mind, the community features extensive landscaping and sustainable development principles.

Project Highlights

  • Total Units: 305 Four Bedroom Townhouses
  • Plot Area: 42,191.55 sq.m.
  • Total Height: 13.6 m
  • Levels: Ground + 3 Floors
  • Type: Residential Townhouses

Amenities & Services

  • Landscaped Green Areas

  • Private Elevators and Sky Roofs
