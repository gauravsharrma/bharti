About Developer

Established as a single entity in 2014, by shareholders with more than 30 years of international experience in property development and construction, Reportage Group started their first project in Abu Dhabi and have subsequently grown in structure, size and number of projects.Reportage began its plan of expansion with its first international project in Egypt- Montenapoleone ,and now Türkiye, Morocco, KSA and Uganda, Pakistan, UK and upcoming international projects. Reportage Community is a serene oasis nestled within the breathtaking expanse of Dubailand, perfectly positioned next to Dubai's most sought-after attractions. This vibrant community offers residents unparalleled access to essential amenities and leisure activities, ensuring that everything you need is just moments away. Surrounded by lush parks and tranquil spaces, Reportage Community provides a harmonious blend of nature and urban living. Families can enjoy leisurely strolls or engage in outdoor activities, all within a picturesque environment that promotes a healthy lifestyle. The thoughtfully designed neighborhood features retail hubs that cater to daily needs, from grocery shopping to dining options, making it incredibly convenient for residents. Additionally, the community is strategically located near major highways, ensuring easy access to the broader city and its myriad attractions, including entertainment venues, cultural landmarks, and shopping destinations.
