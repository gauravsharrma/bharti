About Brabus Island

Strategically positioned within Al Raha Beach, Abu Dhabi; a prestigious waterfront development along the Arabian Gulf’s eastern coast. This prime location ensures residents have easy access to both Abu Dhabi’s city center and major transport routes leading to Dubai.
